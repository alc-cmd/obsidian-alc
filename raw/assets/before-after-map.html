The shift

Prompt Engineering
Memory Engineering

The same model. The same intelligence. Completely different results. Not because you asked better — because your context changed what was possible to ask.

Before — Prompt Engineering

The prompt "I've been reading about AI development timelines and agent workflows. What are the most important trends in AI right now and how should I think about using AI tools effectively?"
You → Prompt → LLM → Generic answer
  • ×
    Starts from zero
    Every conversation begins with no context. The model knows nothing about what you've read, built, or care about.
  • ×
    Generic output
    You get a well-written but surface-level answer that could have been written for anyone.
  • ×
    Nothing compounds
    Close the chat, lose the context. Your next session starts from scratch again.
  • ×
    Prompt-dependent
    Output quality depends entirely on how well you can stuff context into a single message.
  • ×
    No connections
    Can't synthesize across your sources. Doesn't know that article A contradicts article B.
vs

After — Memory Engineering

The prompt "What's the relationship between the AI intelligence explosion thesis and Karpathy's observation about peaky AI capabilities? How does this inform my AI workflow?"
You → Wiki → LLM + Context → Insight
  • ✓
    Persistent context
    28 wiki pages of indexed knowledge. The model reads your index, finds relevant pages, draws on everything you've accumulated.
  • ✓
    Cross-source synthesis
    Connects AI 2027's intelligence explosion to Karpathy's capability gap to your own workflow — references already in place.
  • ✓
    Knowledge compounds
    Every source ingested, every query filed back — the wiki gets richer. Source #7 connects to sources #1, #3, and #5 automatically.
  • ✓
    Architecture-dependent
    Output quality depends on your knowledge architecture, not your prompting skill. Build once, benefit forever.
  • ✓
    New questions emerge
    The wiki surface area grows, revealing questions you couldn't have asked before. The context changes what's possible to think.

The model didn't get smarter.
The context changed what was possible to ask.
