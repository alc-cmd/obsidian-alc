Today's trading agent, in five steps

AI talks.

Today's agents ship the first two steps — reading the chart, writing a paragraph. The rest lands on you: open the exchange, size the position, send the order. Three manual steps per signal. That is not agency.

Thesis

The agent that ships closes the loop. Not better prose — routed orders, signed transactions, confirmed fills. Everything after “text output” is product work most teams have not done.
