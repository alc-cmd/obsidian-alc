# Core Contributor Roles

## Proposed Structure
- Remove inactive Content Creators who haven't posted for a long time
- Core Contributors get **3 consecutive months** of XP and plans (vs monthly Creator rewards)
- Rewards: 30k XP, private channel with Core Team, early access to updates/products, higher commission privileges
- Requirement: at least 3 posts per month
- Removal after 3 months (or earlier) if no posts or community activity
- Continuous filtering to ensure meaningful, sustainable contributions

## Season 2 PFP Event
To celebrate Season 2: users who create their own Minara PFP and use it as Discord PFP earn a chance at 2,000 XP. No cap on how many times users can participate.
