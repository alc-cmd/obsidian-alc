## What actually works (patterns from the data)

1. **Tool-discovery posts with a number receipt beat everything else.** "155k stars", "5k+ stars", "1 in 7" — concrete numerator/denominator in the first line. 12–18% ER consistently.
2. **Personal stack declarations convert.** "ChatGPT is my doctor. Claude is my coworker. Cursor is my dev." — 16.3% ER, 4 profile visits. Punchy identity > long explainer.
3. **Media-only posts can 10x your reach.** The Apr 10 media post hit 1,552 imp — more than the next 3 posts combined. 143 detail expands means the visual pulls people in.
4. **Timely reaction beats evergreen.** "Codex should be cooked now", "Anthropic has the final pieces", "Loveable CEO reaction" all outperform philosophical posts like "Read slow".
5. **Replies are dead weight for reach.** 31 replies = 60% of posting effort, 16% of impressions, 0 new followers directly attributable. Cap them.
6. **Follower conversion is low.** 9 new follows from 5,910 impressions = **0.15% follower conversion**. Content resonates with existing followers; it's not breaking into new feeds.

## What to change

- **Ship 1 original/day minimum** (W15 proved this works — 22 posts drove 6 follows).
- **Open with a number.** "155k", "1 in 7", "10-min setup". Not adjectives.
- **Drop the reply-heavy days.** Replies under 50 impressions almost always. Save the reach for originals.
- **Always attach media** to the hero post of the day. Media-only post outperformed every text post this quarter.
- **Lean into the Minara/Strategy Studio angle with data.** "1 in 7 TV strategies" is the template — real crawl, real number, real claim.
