## Patterns (extracted into concepts)

1. **[[Number-First Hook]]** — opening with a concrete number drives 12–18% ER vs ~4% for adjective hooks.
2. **[[Stack Declaration Post]]** — identity-declaration format ("X is my Y") outperforms explainers for profile conversion.
3. **[[Reply Reach Trap]]** — 60% of output, 16% of reach, 0 attributable follows. Cap at 3/day.
4. **Media > text for reach.** The Apr 10 media-only post hit 1,552 impressions — more than the next three posts combined.
5. **Timely reaction beats evergreen.** "Codex should be cooked", "Anthropic has the final pieces", "Loveable CEO reaction" outperform philosophical posts at the same impression floor.
6. **Follower conversion is the underpulled lever.** 0.15% conversion means content resonates with existing followers; distribution isn't reaching new feeds. Fix = volume, not craft.

## What to change (recommendations)

- **Ship ≥1 original per day.** W15 proved the model (22 posts → 6 follows).
- **Every hero post opens with a number.** See [[Number-First Hook]].
- **Hard cap 3 replies/day.** See [[Reply Reach Trap]].
- **Attach media to the daily hero post.** Image or video.
- **Keep leaning into [[Minara]] data posts.** "1 in 7 TV strategies" is the template — real crawl, real number.
- **Ship a [[Stack Declaration Post]] once a month.** Rotate the tools in the declaration.
