# Andrej Karpathy

AI researcher, educator, and practitioner. Former director of AI at Tesla, former researcher at OpenAI. Known for making deep learning accessible through tutorials, courses (Stanford CS231n), and YouTube videos. Active proponent of using LLMs for knowledge management.

## Key Facts
- Pioneered the "LLM Knowledge Base" workflow: using LLMs to compile and maintain structured markdown wikis from raw sources
- Uses Obsidian as the viewing frontend, with schema stored in `AGENTS.md`
- Advocates keeping the setup simple: nested directories of `.md` and `.png` files, no complex tooling
- Coined the approach of "vibe coding" small tools for LLM use
