# Dan Koe

Writer, content creator, and advocate for essay writing as a tool for thinking and meaning-making. Argues that genuine thinking in public is both a civilizational need and a viable career path.

## Key Facts
- Promotes the distinction between "fast content" (engagement-optimized) and "slow content" (essays, genuine thinking)
- Draws on Daniel Schmachtenberger's metacrisis framework and Csikszentmihalyi's flow theory
- Sees the "meaning economy" as an emerging opportunity for writers
- Pro-AI but argues AI exhausts creativity and cannot produce genuine essays
