# Daniel Kokotajlo

Former OpenAI researcher, lead author of [[AI 2027]]. Named to TIME100 list. Known for his AI predictions, including a 2021 forecast ("What 2026 Looks Like") that successfully predicted chain-of-thought reasoning, inference scaling, chip export controls, and $100M training runs — all before ChatGPT launched.

## Key Facts
- Former OpenAI researcher who left over safety concerns
- Featured in New York Times and TIME100
- Co-authored AI 2027 with [[Eli Lifland]], Thomas Larsen, Romeo Dean, and Scott Alexander
- Previous forecasting track record considered strong by the AI safety community
