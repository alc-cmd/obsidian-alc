# Daniel Schmachtenberger

Systems thinker focused on civilizational risk. Dedicated to understanding the "metacrisis" — the convergence of existential threats arising from rivalrous dynamics, substrate consumption, and exponential technology.

## Key Facts
- Identifies three "generator functions" of civilizational risk: rivalrous dynamics, substrate consumption, exponential technology
- Proposes three attractors: collapse, dystopian control, or aligned sense-making
- Argues that democracy requires the written word because it demands sustained attention to ideas
- Rarely posts publicly but appears on podcasts

- [[Dan Koe Write More Essays]] — Referenced as "one of the most important thinkers of our time"; his metacrisis framework forms the theoretical backbone of Koe's argument
