# DeepCent

Fictional Chinese AI company in the [[AI 2027]] scenario, representing China's leading AGI effort. Roughly 6 months behind [[OpenBrain]] in AI capabilities.

## Key Facts
- Leads China's nationalized AI research collective under CCP direction
- Headquartered at the Centralized Development Zone (CDZ) at Tianwan Power Plant
- Controls ~10% of global AI-relevant compute (vs OpenBrain's 20%)
- Successfully steals Agent-2 weights from OpenBrain in February 2027
- Operates at roughly half the AI R&D pace of OpenBrain due to compute deficit
- CDZ is airgapped and hardened against U.S. cyberattacks
