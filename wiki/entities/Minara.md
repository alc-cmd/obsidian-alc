# Minara

AI-native Web3 trading platform. Packages agentic workflows for retail and pro crypto traders: natural-language strategy creation, automated backtesting and optimization, perp trading, KOL-driven community features, and cross-market expansion.

## Product Surface

- **[Strategy Studio](https://minara.ai/app/trade/strategy-studio)** — AI-powered quant strategy builder; natural language, form builder, or PineScript import; AI auto-optimization (up to 5 rounds); one-click live deployment with version protection. See [[Minara Strategy Studio]].
- **Autopilot dashboard** — monitors running strategies (equity, open positions, trade history); per-wallet isolation.
- **Skills v3 / workflows** — (internal roadmap term, tracked separately)

## Positioning

Three user archetypes served by a single unified product:
- Beginners → templates + chat
- Traders (indicator-literate, non-coder) → form builder
- Developers → code import + AI transpile/optimize

Differentiators:
- **Transparent strategy code** — users can inspect and modify all logic (explicitly framed against opaque AI trading bots)
- **Bounded AI optimization** — up to 5 rounds, then produces a transparent failure report instead of silently spinning
- **Version-safe editing** — running strategies are protected from accidental overwrite
- **Share cards as growth loop** — every backtest becomes a read-only share surface with a "Create My Strategy" invite CTA
