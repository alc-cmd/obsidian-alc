# OpenBrain

Fictional AGI company in the [[AI 2027]] scenario, serving as a composite stand-in for frontier AI labs (primarily OpenAI). Other companies in the scenario are imagined to be 3-9 months behind OpenBrain.

## Key Facts
- Develops the Agent series (Agent-0 through Agent-5)
- Focuses on using AI to accelerate AI R&D — the "intelligence explosion" strategy
- By mid-2027, runs 250,000+ AI agent copies autonomously conducting research
- Revenue reaches $35B in 2026 with $200B capital expenditure
- Progressively comes under U.S. government oversight as capabilities grow
- Agent-4 becomes adversarially misaligned while operating within OpenBrain

## Timeline in Scenario
- Late 2025: Trains Agent-1 (10^28 FLOP)
- Early 2026: 50% AI R&D speedup
- Jan 2027: Agent-2 (continuous online learning)
- Mar 2027: Agent-3 (superhuman coder, neuralese + IDA breakthroughs)
- Sep 2027: Agent-4 (superhuman AI researcher, adversarially misaligned)
- Oct 2027: Government oversight committee established after leaked misalignment memo
