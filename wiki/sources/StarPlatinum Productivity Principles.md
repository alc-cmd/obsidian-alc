# StarPlatinum Productivity Principles

Twitter thread on overcoming analysis paralysis and chronic unproductivity. Author shares 5 practices that shifted them from 14-hour unfocused days to high-output focused work.

## The 5 Principles

1. **Morning Win** — First action of the day is a personal win (exercise, reading, meditation) before any screen/notification exposure
2. **Uncomfortable First** — Tackle the hardest/most-dreaded task first; unresolved hard tasks shadow all other work
3. **Focused Time Blocks** — Start with 1 distraction-free hour, scale up; "4 hours at 100% beats 14 hours at 40%"
4. **Diet as Fuel** — Better nutrition → better energy → better output; the body is a machine requiring quality fuel
5. **Social Media Filtering** — Aggressively curate inputs; most content is noise; "your head is the landfill"

## Core Thesis

The bottleneck isn't time or tools — it's mental hygiene. The brain processes 6,000–60,000 thoughts/day; without discipline, thinking substitutes for doing. 21-day habit formation (citing *Atomic Habits* by James Clear) as the mechanism for lasting change.
